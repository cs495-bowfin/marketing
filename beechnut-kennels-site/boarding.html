Boarding

Canine Boarding

Our all-inclusive boarding ensures your canine stays safe, comfortable, and entertained with temperature-controlled rooms, daily treats, and up to six hours of supervised playtime each day. With cozy bedding, daily treats, and plenty of love from our caring staff, your pup will feel right at home!

Accommodations

Temperature-controlled rooms

7’x8′ for large canines
4’x5′ for small canines

Attached outdoor run

Freely accessible by dog door when weather permits

Supervised playtime up to 6 hours daily

Endless pets and cuddles from our loving staff!

Feeding and medicine administration

Includes daily treats!

Indoor playrooms and Astroturf play yards

Enriched with furniture, equipment, and toys

Requirements
  1. Spayed or Neutered if over 6 months of age.
  2. Required Vaccinations must be up to date: Rabies, Distemper, Bordetella, Canine Bivalent Flu (Puppies under 4 months are eligible 2 weeks after their Bordatella and 2nd Distemper vaccines have been given)
  3. Vaccination Records must be provided at least 3 days prior to a visit.
  4. Pets must be on a topical or oral flea preventative or a Seresto brand flea collar.
  5. Pets must socialize safely on the Trial Day to become eligible for services.
Trial Day

Your dog’s first day with Beechnut is known as their evaluation or trial day. During this day your dog will attend a full day of daycare with the oversight of highly experienced managers and trainers. We will ease your pet in to help them acclimate with the staff, sights/sounds of the facility, and fellow furry friends at their own pace, whatever that may be. Our managers spend the whole day evaluating your pet’s comfort as they choose to follow their curiosities to give us deeper insight on your pets personality, personal needs, and play-style. The full day gives your pet adequate time to settle in and allows us the opportunity to genuinely assess if our program will be a good fit for your pet immediately or if they need further acclimation days, training, or if other models of care should be suggested. This is beneficial to your pet as well as our facility and staff by giving both parties a slow and proper introduction to a new and fun relationship.

  • Trial day is performed during a full day of daycare by appointment only. Appointments are available Monday through Thursday each week.
  • The cost of a trial day is $45 per dog including a mid-day phone call update and report card with photos.
  • Drop-off is between 7AM – 10AM and pick-up is between 2PM – 6PM. We recommend pick up between 2PM – 3PM for nervous pups!
Activities

Beechnut is a socialized boarding facility that offers your pet up to 6 hours of participation in social play time with other boarding and daycare pets. This activity is included in our all-inclusive flat rate. The play groups are set up by size and personality. The schedules are customized to suit age, energy level, and personality to ensure it is an enjoyable experience. Our play yards are always supervised, managed with positive attitudes, and enriched with toys and play equipment.

Feeding and Health

CUSTOMIZED FEEDING

Beechnut does require that you provide your own pet’s meals, and we always feed according to your instructions up to 3 times a day. We also monitor and record your pet’s intake and contact you if there are concerns about appetite.

Please pack food in pre-measured baggies for each meal to eliminate measuring errors. We do have a refrigerator for perishable dog food.

MEDICATION

We administer medications to your pet if you provide the original medication container with the medication name, dosage, and instructions on the label. Please be prepared to explain the purpose of the medication, duration of use, and any side effects that need to be monitored.

*We do not accept responsibility for a pet that has life-sustaining medication, but we are happy to administer maintenance medications at no additional fee.

*We do not administer insulin injections.

HEALTH CHECK

We review and record how each pet is doing on each morning of their stay. We make adjustments to their schedule, feeding, and medications based on any changes or concerns. We always contact you with any concerns/questions and keep you updated on your pet’s wellness. You can trust that we care.

Luggage Options

Please ensure you bring familiar items to your pet that you have already deemed “safe” for them to have unattended. Please do not bring your favorite items in case they are lost or destroyed. Items listed are optional—you can bring your pet without any “luggage.”

  • Up to 3 meals per day of your own food is required to be in pre-measured baggies.
  • Treats (optional). Chews will be left with pet unattended and are provided at your own risk.
  • 1 item of washable bedding (optional)
  • Up to 2 toys (optional)
  • Medications/Treatments (if needed). Must be provided in original container.

*Do not put medicine in food baggies.

*Label all items with last name.

Canine Boarding Rates

Rate per Day: $65

2 Pet Discount: $2.50

3 Pet Discount: $3.00

*All pets staying past 10 AM will be charged for that day.

*3-day minimum charge applies for peak season weekends/holidays.

*A discount of $2.50/day per pet will be applied when 2 pets share a room.

*A discount of $3.00/day per pet will be applied when 3 pets share a room.

*All pets boarding 5+ days require a clean up bath at the owner’s expense.

Feline Boarding

We offer a quiet building completely separate from the canine facility where felines can relax on a perch in their two story enclosure. Our cats have plenty of natural light, time to roam and play, and individual attention.

Accommodations

  • Feline Building separate from Canine facility
  • Two Perch Enclosure
  • Individual, hourly rotations to feline playroom that features a cat walk and various enrichment toys and furniture
  • Heated/Air Conditioned
  • Bedding and Litter Provided (or you may bring your own)
  • Medication Needs
  • Customized feedings up to 3 times a day

*We will not accept responsibility for a pet that has life-sustaining medication, but we are happy to administer maintenance medications at no additional fee.

*We do not administer insulin injections.

Vaccinations & Flea Prevention

All cats must be up-to-date on the following vaccinations:

  • Rabies
  • Feline Distempter
  • Feline’s must be on a topical or oral flea preventative or a Seresto brand flea collar.

*Outdoor cats must also provide proof of Feline Leukemia vaccination.

Feline Boarding Rates

Rate per Day: $30

*All pets picked up after 10 AM will be charged for that day.

*3-day minimum charge applies for peak season weekends/holidays.
